Privacy Policy — SleepRitual

Effective Date: 2026-03-02 | Developer: Daisuke Arai / Anicca

1. Introduction

SleepRitual is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Short version: SleepRitual stores your sleep ritual data locally on your device. We do not sell your data. We do not have a backend server. Your ritual steps and streaks never leave your device unless noted below.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

DataWhatWhy
Ritual stepsYour custom bedtime routine stepsTo display and track your ritual
Streak recordsConsecutive nights completedTo show your streak counter
App settingsNotification time, onboarding statusTo remember your preferences

All of the above is stored exclusively in iOS UserDefaults on your device. It is never transmitted to any server.

2.2 Analytics Data (Mixpanel)

We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous analytics. This helps us improve the product. Mixpanel does NOT receive your ritual step names, streak history, or any personally identifiable information.

Mixpanel Privacy Policy

2.3 Subscription Data (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat processes anonymous subscriber IDs and subscription status only. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or ritual content.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy

2.4 Notification Permissions

If you enable bedtime reminders, we use iOS UserNotifications to send local notifications. This data stays on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

SleepRitual is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us. To delete locally stored data, simply delete the App.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the Effective Date.
