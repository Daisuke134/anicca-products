# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

**App:** AffirmFlow
**Developer:** Anicca

## Overview

AffirmFlow ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

**Key Point:** AffirmFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle. All affirmation generation happens on your device using Apple's on-device AI. Your personal thoughts and affirmations never leave your phone.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and SwiftData:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| Focus Areas | Personalize your affirmations | Device only |
| Favorite Affirmations | Allow you to revisit saved affirmations | Device only |
| Affirmation History | Show past affirmations | Device only |
| App Preferences | Remember your settings (theme, notifications) | Device only |
| Subscription Status | Determine feature access | Device only |

**This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.**

### 2. Subscription and Payment Information

When you make a purchase through the App Store, Apple processes your payment. We receive:
- Confirmation that a subscription is active
- Subscription type (monthly or annual)
- Anonymous transaction identifiers

We do NOT receive:
- Your credit card number
- Your Apple ID
- Your real name or billing address

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for details.

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect your name, email, or contact information
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your photos, camera, or microphone
- We do NOT use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
- We do NOT use any form of tracking or cross-app tracking
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

---

## How We Use Your Information

All data stored by AffirmFlow is used solely to:
1. Generate personalized affirmations based on your selected focus areas
2. Display your widget on your home screen
3. Save and retrieve your favorite affirmations
4. Manage your subscription status

---

## Third-Party Services

### Apple (Required)

- **App Store & In-App Purchases:** Subscription processing
- **Apple Intelligence:** On-device AI for affirmation generation (data stays on device)
- **WidgetKit:** Home screen widget display (data stays on device)

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

---

## Data Retention

- All app data is stored locally on your device
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored data
- Subscription status may be retained by Apple as part of your purchase history

---

## Your Rights

### Delete Your Data

You can delete all app data at any time by:
1. Deleting the AffirmFlow app from your device
2. All local data will be permanently removed

### Cancel Your Subscription

To cancel your subscription:
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select AffirmFlow and tap "Cancel Subscription"

---

## Data Protection by Design

AffirmFlow is built with privacy-first principles:

- **On-Device Processing:** All AI features use Apple's on-device models. Your affirmations are generated locally and never sent to servers.
- **No Account Required:** You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.
- **No Analytics:** We do not collect usage analytics or telemetry.
- **No Ads:** We do not show advertisements or use ad tracking.

---

## Children's Privacy

AffirmFlow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. No personal information is required to use the app.

## Regional Rights

### For Users in the European Union (GDPR)

- **Lawful Basis:** Our processing is based on the contract you have with us when you use the app
- **Data Controller:** Anicca
- **Data Stored:** Only on your local device
- **Your Rights:** Access, rectification, erasure, data portability, objection

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect personal information, you have full control over your data.

### For Users in California (CCPA)

- We do NOT sell your personal information
- We do NOT share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
- You have the right to delete your data (delete the app)
