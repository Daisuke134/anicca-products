## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Pain Area Preferences:** When you select pain areas during onboarding (e.g., neck, back, shoulders, wrists), these selections are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.
- **Timer Settings:** Your preferred break intervals and stretch durations are stored locally on your device.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Subscription Status:** We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat collects anonymized transaction data to process your purchases. See [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).
- **Crash Data & Diagnostics:** Apple may collect crash logs and diagnostics if you opt in via iOS Settings. We do not use any third-party crash reporting SDKs.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, or account credentials (no sign-in required).
- We do **not** collect location data.
- We do **not** collect health data via HealthKit.
- We do **not** use advertising identifiers (IDFA) or tracking SDKs.
- We do **not** use analytics SDKs (no Mixpanel, Firebase Analytics, or similar).
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage Only:** Your app preferences (pain areas, timer settings, onboarding completion) are stored exclusively on your device using UserDefaults.
- **No Cloud Sync:** We do not sync your data to any external servers.
- **No Account Required:** The app does not require sign-in, so no credentials are stored.
- **Encryption:** Data in transit (subscription verification) uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **On-Device Data:** Stored until you delete the app or reset your preferences within the app.
- **Subscription Data:** Managed by Apple and RevenueCat per their respective retention policies.
