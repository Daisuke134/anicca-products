# Privacy Policy — SleepRitual

**Effective Date**: 2026-03-02
**App**: SleepRitual
**Developer**: Daisuke Arai / Anicca

---

## 1. Introduction

SleepRitual ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

**Short version**: SleepRitual stores your sleep ritual data locally on your device. We do not sell your data. We do not have a backend server. Your ritual steps and streaks never leave your device unless noted below.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally (On Your Device Only)

| Data | What | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| Ritual steps | Your custom bedtime routine steps | To display and track your ritual |
| Streak records | Consecutive nights completed | To show your streak counter |
| App settings | Notification time, onboarding status | To remember your preferences |

**Storage**: All of the above is stored exclusively in iOS UserDefaults on your device. It is never transmitted to any server.

### 2.2 Analytics Data (Mixpanel)

We use **Mixpanel** to collect anonymous analytics about how users interact with the App. This helps us improve the product.

| Event | Data Collected |
|-------|---------------|
| App opens | Anonymous device identifier |
| Onboarding progress | Step reached (no personal info) |
| Paywall viewed / purchased | Anonymous purchase event |
| Ritual completed | Completion event (not your ritual content) |
| Streak milestones | Milestone number |

**What Mixpanel does NOT receive**: Your ritual step names, your streak history, any personally identifiable information.

Mixpanel Privacy Policy: https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy

### 2.3 Subscription Data (RevenueCat)

We use **RevenueCat** to manage subscriptions and in-app purchases. RevenueCat processes:
- Your App Store transaction data (handled by Apple)
- Anonymous subscriber ID
- Subscription status (active/expired/trial)

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or ritual content.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### 2.4 Notification Permissions

If you enable bedtime reminders, we use iOS **UserNotifications** to send you a local notification at your chosen time. This data stays on your device — we do not process it on any server.

## 5. Children's Privacy

SleepRitual is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us at the address below.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Local data** (UserDefaults): Retained until you delete the App or clear App data.
- **Analytics data** (Mixpanel): Retained per Mixpanel's data retention policy (typically 5 years for free plan).
- **Subscription data** (RevenueCat): Retained per RevenueCat's policy.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws:

- **Access**: Request what data we hold about you.
- **Deletion**: Delete the App to remove all locally stored data. For analytics, contact us to request deletion from Mixpanel.
- **Opt-out of analytics**: Contact us at the address below.
