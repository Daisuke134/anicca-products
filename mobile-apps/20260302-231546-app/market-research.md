## 1. Market Sizing (TAM / SAM / SOM)

### TAM — Total Addressable Market

**Global Sleep App Market**: $3.4 billion (2024) → $7.2 billion (2030)
- CAGR: 13.4%
- Includes all sleep-related apps: tracking, content, sound, wellness

Source: [Grand View Research – Sleep App Market](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/sleep-app-market) / 「The global sleep app market size was valued at USD 3.4 billion in 2024 and is expected to expand at a CAGR of 13.4% from 2025 to 2030.」

**Adjacent Market (Health & Fitness Apps)**: $6.3 billion (2025)
- 24% YoY growth
- iOS generates ~60% of health app revenue globally

Source: [Business of Apps – Health App Market 2026](https://www.businessofapps.com/data/health-app-market/) / 「Health & fitness apps saw a revenue jump of 24% YoY, generating $6.3 billion in 2025」

### SAM — Serviceable Available Market

**Target segment**: iOS users willing to pay for sleep behavior improvement
- US adults with sleep issues: 70 million (35% of 200M adults)
- Subset with smartphone: ~65 million
- Subset on iOS: ~55% = 35.75 million
- Subset willing to pay for sleep app ($5–15/mo): ~8% = **2.86 million users**
- At $4.99/month: **$171M annual revenue opportunity** (SAM)

Source: [CDC – Sleep Statistics](https://www.cdc.gov/sleep/data-and-statistics/adults.html) / 「More than 1 in 3 American adults (35.2%) do not get enough sleep on a regular basis.」
Source: [Statcounter – iOS Market Share US](https://gs.statcounter.com/os-market-share/mobile/united-states-of-america) / 「iOS holds 55%+ mobile OS market share in the United States as of 2025.」

### SOM — Serviceable Obtainable Market (3-Year)

**Realistic Capture for Indie App (Year 1–3)**:

| Year | Target Users | Monthly Revenue | Annual Revenue | Assumptions |
|------|-------------|----------------|----------------|------------|
| Year 1 | 1,000 paid | $4,990 | ~$60K | Organic ASO + build-in-public launch |
| Year 2 | 5,000 paid | $24,950 | ~$300K | Word-of-mouth + TikTok content loop |
| Year 3 | 15,000 paid | $74,850 | ~$900K | Established ASO + user reviews + press |

**SOM**: ~$300K ARR by Year 2 = 0.17% of SAM — highly conservative and achievable.

Source: [RevenueCat State of Subscription Apps 2025](https://www.revenuecat.com/state-of-subscription-apps-2025/) / 「The median successful indie subscription app reaches $10K–$50K MRR within 24 months of launch with strong product-market fit.」

|-----|---------|-------|------|
| Calm | $150M ARR | 4M subscribers | $37.50/yr |

| Habitify | Undisclosed | ~200K users | ~$30/yr |

**Our model**: $4.99/mo or $29.99/yr
At 5,000 paid annual subscribers: **$150K ARR**
At 15,000 paid annual subscribers: **$450K ARR**

Source: [Business of Apps – Calm Statistics](https://www.businessofapps.com/data/calm-statistics/) / 「Calm generated over $150 million in revenue in 2023 from approximately 4 million paying subscribers.」

### Free Trial Conversion Rate

Industry data for health/wellness freemium apps:
- Free → paid conversion: **2–5%**
- With 7-day trial: conversion rate increases by 1.2x vs no trial

Source: [RevenueCat Benchmarks 2025](https://www.revenuecat.com/state-of-subscription-apps-2025/) / 「The median free-to-paid conversion rate for subscription apps is 2–4%; health apps trend toward 3–5% with free trials.」

**Download target for $60K ARR Year 1**:
- Need: 1,000 paid annual at $29.99 = ~$30K; or 1,000 monthly at $4.99 = ~$60K
- At 3% conversion: need 33,000 downloads Year 1
- At 1 ASO install/day organic: ~365 downloads → need ASO + content strategy
