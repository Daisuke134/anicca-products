## Codebase Patterns

- App naming: [BrandName]: [Descriptor] format (e.g., "MindSnap: AI Daily Check-in")
- Bundle ID pattern: com.anicca.[appname_lowercase]
- Foundation Models + no-backend = fastest path to AI-differentiated app in 1 day
- Subscription sweet spot: $4.99/mo or $29.99/yr for wellness/productivity apps (2026 data)
- Free trial > hard paywall: 7-day trial converts better per BusinessOfApps 2026
