## Core Value Proposition

**One sentence:** 30-second daily mood check-in with on-device AI reflection prompts — zero account required, zero data sent to cloud.

**Problem solved:** Users want AI-powered self-awareness tools but distrust cloud apps and can't commit 5+ minutes to traditional journaling.
