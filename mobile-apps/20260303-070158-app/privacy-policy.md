# Privacy Policy — MindSnap

**Last updated:** March 3, 2026

## Overview

MindSnap ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core feature. **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

## Data Collection

MindSnap does **not** collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Health or fitness data
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Device identifiers
- Any other personal information

## Data Storage

All data you enter (mood check-ins, notes) is stored **exclusively on your device** using Apple's secure local storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any cloud service operated by us
- Is not accessible to MindSnap or any third party

## Third-Party Services

MindSnap uses **RevenueCat** to process subscription payments. RevenueCat processes payment information through Apple's App Store. We do not receive your payment details.

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

## Apple's On-Device AI (Foundation Models)

MindSnap uses Apple's Foundation Models framework to generate reflection prompts on your device. This processing happens entirely on-device. No journal entries or personal data are sent to Apple or any other server for AI processing.

## Children's Privacy

MindSnap is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Changes

We will update this policy if our practices change. The latest version is always at this URL.
