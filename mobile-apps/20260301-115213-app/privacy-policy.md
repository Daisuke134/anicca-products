# Privacy Policy — Micro Mood

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview

Micro Mood ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Micro Mood iOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Mood entries**: The mood ratings (1–5 scale) and optional notes you log within the app
- **Account information**: If you create an account (optional), we collect your email address

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage data**: Anonymous analytics data about how you use the app (powered by Mixpanel)
- **Device information**: Device type, iOS version, and app version for debugging purposes
- **Subscription status**: Managed through RevenueCat to provide premium features

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, location, photos, or contacts
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising

## How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Provide AI mood insights | Your mood entries (processed on-device or securely on our servers) |
| Improve the app | Anonymous usage analytics |
| Process subscriptions | Subscription status via RevenueCat |
| Customer support | Email address (if provided) |

## Data Storage and Security

- Mood data is stored locally on your device using CoreData
- We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmitted to our servers
- We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy |
| Mixpanel | Anonymous analytics | https://mixpanel.com/legal/privacy-policy |
| Apple StoreKit | In-app purchases | https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy |

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics in the app settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@anicca.app

## Children's Privacy

Micro Mood is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email.
