## Codebase Patterns

- Foundation Models (iOS 18+): on-device LLM, free inference, no API key required. Use for all AI features. No backend needed.
- SwiftData: use over CoreData for new projects (iOS 17+). Simple schema, works with SwiftUI @Query.
- RevenueCat: keys from Info.plist, NOT ProcessInfo.processInfo.environment (CRITICAL RULE #17).
- Soft paywall: SwiftUI custom PaywallView + Purchases.shared.purchase(package:). RevenueCatUI.PaywallView is BANNED.
- HealthKit: read-only sleep data integration requires NSHealthShareUsageDescription in Info.plist.
