# Privacy Policy — BreathStory

**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026
**Developer:** Anicca / Daisuke Takeuchi
**Contact:** keiodaisuke@gmail.com
**App:** BreathStory — Guided Breathing Stories (com.anicca.breathstory)

---

## Summary

BreathStory is an offline-first app. We do **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. There is no backend server, no account system, and no analytics SDK.

---

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

BreathStory stores the following data **locally on your device only** using Apple's UserDefaults:

| Data | Purpose | Shared? |
|------|---------|---------|
| Subscription status (active/inactive) | Unlock premium stories | Never |
| Streak count (consecutive days used) | In-app display only | Never |
| Onboarding completion flag | Skip intro on relaunch | Never |

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server.

---

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | Purchase receipt (anonymized transaction ID) |
| **Apple App Store** | Payment processing | Your Apple ID (managed by Apple) |

**RevenueCat** receives your App Store purchase receipt to validate subscriptions. RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. See [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

We do NOT use:
- Analytics SDKs (no Mixpanel, Firebase, Amplitude, etc.)
- Advertising networks (no AdMob, AppLovin, etc.)
- App Tracking Transparency (no IDFA collection)
- Crash reporting services (no Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)

---

## Audio & Speech

BreathStory uses Apple's built-in **AVSpeechSynthesizer** and **AVPlayer** for narration and ambient sound. All audio processing happens on-device. No audio data is transmitted anywhere.

---

## Children's Privacy

BreathStory is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, COPPA requirements are inherently satisfied.

---

## Data Retention

We do not retain any data on our servers because we have no servers. Local device data (UserDefaults) persists until you delete the app or reset your device.

---

## Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete on our end. To remove all local app data, delete BreathStory from your device.

**GDPR (EU users):** We have no lawful basis issue since no personal data is processed.
**CCPA (California users):** We do not sell personal information because we do not collect it.

---

## Subscription & Payments

All payments are processed by Apple. We do not see or store your payment information. To manage or cancel your subscription, go to: **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → BreathStory**.
