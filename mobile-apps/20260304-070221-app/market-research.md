## 4. Entry Barriers

| Barrier | Level | BreathStory Position |
|---------|-------|---------------------|
| Technical implementation (breathing app) | **Low** — SwiftUI + AVSpeechSynthesizer | Advantage: AVSpeechSynthesizer eliminates TTS API cost |
| Content creation (breathing stories) | **Medium** — requires quality copywriting | Mitigated: Pre-written scripts, 5 MVP stories |
| App Store discoverability | **Medium** — crowded Health & Fitness | ASO with long-tail keywords ("breathing stories", "narrative breathwork") |
| Brand recognition | **High** — Calm/Headspace have 10+ years | Niche positioning bypasses direct competition |
| User switching costs | **Low** — wellness apps are easily replaced | Users try multiple apps; low commitment |
| Development cost | **Very Low** — no backend, no APIs | Solo dev advantage: $0 ongoing infrastructure cost |

**Overall Entry Barrier: Low** for the narrative-breathing niche specifically.
