## 1. Overview

Chi Daily is a 100% offline app. All processing occurs on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

| Data Type | Collected | Stored | Shared |
|-----------|-----------|--------|--------|
| Personal information (name, email) | No | No | No |
| Location data | No | No | No |
| Health data | Yes (HealthKit write-only) | On-device only | No |
| Check-in answers | Yes | On-device only | No |
| Subscription status | Yes (via RevenueCat) | On-device only | No |
| Analytics / tracking | No | No | No |
| Device identifiers | No | No | No |

### Data Collected and Stored Locally

- **Daily check-in responses**: Your 5 constitution check-in answers and AI-generated recommendations are stored locally using SwiftData. This data never leaves your device.
- **HealthKit**: With your permission, Chi Daily writes your constitution type as a tag. We do not read any other HealthKit data.
- **Subscription status**: RevenueCat SDK stores your purchase receipt on-device and communicates with RevenueCat servers solely to verify your subscription. No health or check-in data is transmitted.
- **App preferences**: Stored locally via UserDefaults (notification time, language setting).

## 4. Apple HealthKit

Chi Daily requests permission to write health data (constitution type) to HealthKit. This data is stored on your device and managed by Apple HealthKit. We do not read your HealthKit data. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices.

---

## 5. Subscriptions and Payments

Payments are processed by Apple. Chi Daily does not receive or store your payment information. RevenueCat SDK verifies subscription status using anonymous receipt validation. No personally identifiable information is shared with RevenueCat.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Chi Daily is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us at keiodaisuke@gmail.com.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since Chi Daily collects no personal data tied to your identity, most data rights requests do not apply. However:

- **California (CCPA)**: We do not sell your personal information.
- **European Union (GDPR)**: We process no personal data. No consent is required for offline-only apps.
- **Japan (APPI)**: We store only locally processed data on your device.

To request deletion of locally stored check-in data, go to iOS Settings > Chi Daily > Delete All Data, or uninstall the app.

---

## 8. Data Retention

All locally stored data (check-in history, preferences) remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. RevenueCat retains anonymous purchase records per their own retention policy.

---

## 9. Security

All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData encryption and iOS device encryption. No network transmission of personal data occurs.
