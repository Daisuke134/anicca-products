## Competitor 1: Kanpobliss — TCM Self Care

**Source:** [App Store listing](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/kanpobliss-tcm-self-care/id1457916828) + [AppFollow](https://appfollow.io/ios/kanpobliss-tcm-self-care/1457916828)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Category** | Direct (TCM consumer wellness) |
| **Platform** | iOS + Android |
| **Developer** | Haymaker Apps Pte Ltd (Singapore) |
| **Rating** | 5.0 stars / 30 reviews |
| **Pricing** | Free (no subscription detected) |
| **Downloads** | Low (30 reviews = ~3,000 installs estimated) |
| **Last update** | 2019 (abandoned) |

**Features:**
- TCM body condition check → lifestyle/diet advice
- Acupressure points reference
- Diet tips by constitution

**Weaknesses vs Chi Daily:**
- No AI (static rule-based recommendations)
- No daily check-in loop
- No HealthKit integration
- No subscription = no revenue model
- Abandoned since 2019 (no iOS 26 support)
- English only (no Japanese)

**Chi Daily advantage:** Foundation Models AI + daily habit loop + subscription + Japanese market.

## Competitor 4: Calm

**Source:** [Business of Apps Calm Statistics](https://www.businessofapps.com/data/calm-statistics/) + [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/calm/id571800810)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Category** | Aspirational (general wellness, not TCM) |
| **Platform** | iOS + Android |
| **Revenue** | ~$300M ARR (2023) |
| **Subscribers** | 4.5M paying (2023) |
| **Pricing** | $14.99/month / $69.99/year |
| **Rating** | 4.8 stars / 2M+ reviews |
| **Target user** | General wellness, stress/sleep |

**Features:**
- Guided meditation (thousands of sessions)
- Sleep stories (narrated bedtime audio)
- Breathing exercises
- Daily Calm (fresh content daily)

**Weaknesses vs Chi Daily:**
- No TCM framework (Western mindfulness only)
- Cloud-based (data leaves device)
- No personalization by constitution type
- No HealthKit logging
- Price is 3x Chi Daily ($14.99 vs $4.99)

**Chi Daily advantage:** TCM niche with under-served demand + privacy (on-device) + 3x lower price = easier trial conversion.

**What Chi Daily borrows:** Daily fresh content model (Daily Calm → Chi Daily check-in).

## Conclusion

**Chi Daily occupies a genuinely uncontested position:**

| Finding | Evidence |
|---------|---------|
| No paying TCM consumer wellness app exists | Kanpobliss (free, dead), TCM Diagnostics (free, new, Chinese) |
| Practitioner TCM apps validated demand | TCM Clinic Aid has 1,200+ reviews and strong ratings |
| General wellness subscriptions prove WTP | Calm ($300M ARR), Headspace ($195M ARR) at $12.99–14.99/mo |
| $4.99/mo is the sweet spot | [RevenueCat 2025](https://www.revenuecat.com/state-of-subscription-apps-2025/) — highest trial conversion at $5–7/month health |
| Foundation Models = moat | No existing TCM app uses on-device AI |
