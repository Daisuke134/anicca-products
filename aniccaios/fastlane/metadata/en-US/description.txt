Daily affirmations that find you at the moment you need them most.

Anicca is your AI self-care companion — a quiet voice of self-love, manifestation, and inner peace, delivered through beautifully crafted daily cards that arrive exactly when your mind starts to spiral.

Most affirmation apps make you sit down, open them, and read. Anicca is different. It learns your day — when you stay up too late, when self-doubt hits, when anxiety creeps in — and sends a single line of kindness right at that moment. Tap it. Read the full card. Feel a little lighter.

THE PHILOSOPHY

Anicca is a Pali word from Buddhist teaching meaning "impermanence" — the truth that no feeling, no thought, no storm lasts forever. Every notification you get is a small reminder of that. You are not your worst moment. This too will pass.
