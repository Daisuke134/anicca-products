# SignatureCraft — AI-Free Email Signature Generator

## Problem

Creating a professional email signature is tedious. Most people have plain text or ugly formatted signatures that don't reflect their professional brand. Manual HTML/CSS editing is error-prone and most people lack the skills. Existing solutions (WiseStamp $5.80/mo, Newoldstamp $6/mo) are either expensive, bloated with features, or require complex onboarding.

## Differentiation

1. **Modern, fast UI** — Real-time preview as you type (no page refresh)
2. **Zero signup for first signature** — Try before creating account
3. **One-click copy** — Works with Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail instantly
4. **Beautiful templates** — Contemporary designs, not 2015-era styles
5. **Lightweight** — No bloated dashboard, just generate and go
