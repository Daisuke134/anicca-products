Privacy Policy — Micro Mood

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  |  App: Micro Mood  |  Developer: Anicca

Summary

Micro Mood stores all mood data on your device. We do not collect, share, or sell your mood entries, notes, or personal information.

Data We Collect

Data TypeWhere It's StoredShared With
Mood entries & notesYour device (CoreData)Nobody
HealthKit mood dataApple Health (your device)Nobody

Purchase receiptsRevenueCatRevenueCat only

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • The content of your mood entries or notes
  • Location data
  • Contacts or communication data

Analytics (Mixpanel)

We use Mixpanel to understand how the app is used (e.g., how many users open the mood check-in). Mixpanel only receives an anonymous device ID — never your mood data or personal information.

Subscriptions (RevenueCat)

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts to verify your subscription status. No personal mood data is shared with RevenueCat.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you enable HealthKit sync, Micro Mood writes your mood as a mindfulness session to Apple Health. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. We do not read any other HealthKit data.

Data Retention

All mood data is stored locally on your device using CoreData. Deleting the app deletes all your mood data. You can also export your data as CSV from Settings.

Children

Micro Mood is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via app update notes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
