1. **App Name**: AffirmFlow
2. **Pricing**: $2.99/week or $29.99/year (competitive with ThinkUp $39.99, I Am $30)
3. **Primary differentiators**:
   - First AI-native affirmation widget (Foundation Models)

4. **MVP Timeline**: 4 weeks (2 phases)

- Mental Health Apps Market: $8.64B (2026) → $35.29B (2034)

### Learnings for future iterations
- Foundation Models integration is straightforward (~3 lines of code)
- Privacy-first positioning resonates in wellness market
- Widget-first design aligns with 2026 "3-second rule" trend
