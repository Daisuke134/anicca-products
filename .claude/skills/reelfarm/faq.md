## General

### What is ReelFarm?

ReelFarm is an AI-powered TikTok content automation platform that creates and publishes slideshows (image carousels) and videos at scale. You set up an automation once with your product context, hooks, images, and format preferences, and ReelFarm will automatically generate new original content and publish it to your connected TikTok accounts on a set schedule — completely on autopilot. The goal is to drive organic traffic back to your product or website through valuable, evergreen content.

## Billing & Plans

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to the ReelFarm dashboard, click your profile card at the bottom of the sidebar, then click 'Billing' and 'Cancel Subscription'.

### Can I get a refund?

ReelFarm handles refunds on a case-by-case basis. If you need a refund, reach out via the feedback form. Refunds typically take 5–10 business days to appear on your statement. Note: if you have used all of your credits, a refund may not be issued. If you accidentally upgraded or were double-charged, refunds are generally granted for the duplicate charge.

### I was charged twice when upgrading plans. What should I do?

If you upgraded from one plan to another and were charged for both, contact support. Matt will refund the lower-tier charge. Upgrades are not prorated — you will be charged the full price of the new plan at the time of upgrading.

### Are plan upgrades prorated?

No. Upgrades are not prorated. When you upgrade your plan mid-billing cycle, you will be charged the full price of the new plan immediately. If you were recently charged for your old plan and then upgrade, contact support and a refund for the previous charge can typically be issued.

### What is the Growth Plan?

The Growth Plan ($49/month) is designed for users who want to post more frequently. It includes more export credits per month and the ability to run multiple automations and schedule multiple posts per day. It's recommended if you want to post 5 or more times per day.

### What is the Scale/Unlimited Plan?

The Scale (Unlimited) Plan is the top tier, offering unlimited slideshow/video generation, unlimited automations, and unlimited connected TikTok accounts. Note: While you can generate unlimited content, TikTok's API enforces a hard limit of 6 posts published per account per 24 hours via any third-party API — this is a TikTok restriction, not a ReelFarm limitation.

## Credits

### How do credits work?

ReelFarm uses two types of credits: Export Credits and AI Credits. 1 export credit = 1 slideshow created/exported. Slideshows are created either manually via the Slideshow Editor or automatically via your automations (each automated slideshow creation deducts 1 credit). AI Credits are used for AI-powered features like AI Avatars and AI image generation. Sending slideshows to TikTok does NOT cost credits — only creating/exporting them does. Credits reset on your monthly billing date.

### Can I buy extra credits without starting or upgrading my subscription?

Yes! ReelFarm offers one-time purchase credits. You can buy additional export credits without changing your subscription plan. These credits are added to your account instantly and do not expire with the billing cycle. The options are $9 for 10 exports, $19 for 25 exports, $29 for 50 exports, and $49 for 100 exports.

### Why did my automation use all my credits unexpectedly?

Each time your automation creates (generates) a slideshow, it costs 1 export credit. If you set up multiple automations creating multiple slideshows per day, your credits can deplete quickly. For example, 3 automations each set to create 2 slideshows per day will use 6 credits daily. Make sure to review your automation frequency settings so you don't burn through credits faster than expected.

### Why did my automation pause?

Automations automatically pause when you have 0 export credits remaining, because they cannot create slideshows without credits. Once you add more credits (via subscription renewal or a one-time purchase), your automations will resume.

## TikTok Publishing

### My posts aren't showing up in TikTok Drafts — where are they?

This is one of the most common questions! When ReelFarm sends a post as a draft, it does NOT appear in your TikTok 'Drafts' folder. Instead, it shows up in your TikTok app under 'Inbox' → 'System Notifications.' Look there to find all your ReelFarm-sent drafts.

### What is the 'Publish as Draft' setting?

'Publish as Draft' sends your slideshow to TikTok as an unpublished draft, so you can review it and manually publish it yourself from the TikTok app. When this setting is ON, your posts will NOT go live automatically — they will appear in your TikTok Inbox under 'System Notifications.' Turn this setting OFF if you want ReelFarm to auto-publish posts directly to your TikTok feed.

### My automation is set to auto-publish but posts aren't going live — why?

A few possible reasons:

1. Your 'Publish as Draft' setting is turned on in the automation — check and turn it off if you want live posts.
2. Your TikTok account's authentication tokens may have expired — remove and re-add your TikTok account in Settings → Connected Accounts to fix this.
3. TikTok's API limit of 6 posts per account per 24 hours has been reached — additional posts won't go through until the next day.
4. TikTok's API may be experiencing downtime — this is outside ReelFarm's control.

### How many posts can I publish to TikTok per day via ReelFarm?

TikTok's official API enforces a hard limit of 6 posts per account per 24-hour period for any third-party app (including ReelFarm). This is a TikTok restriction, not a ReelFarm limitation. If you exceed 6 posts in 24 hours, additional posts will fail to publish. You can always download the slideshow images and post manually to get around this limit if needed.

### What is the TikTok API post limit per day?

TikTok's official API allows a maximum of 6 posts per account per 24-hour window when publishing through any third-party app (including ReelFarm). This is a TikTok-enforced restriction and cannot be bypassed by ReelFarm. If you need to post more than 6 times per day on a single account, you can download the slideshow images and post the remaining ones manually through the TikTok app.

### Does posting through ReelFarm hurt my TikTok reach?

No. ReelFarm uses the official TikTok API, so posting through ReelFarm does not negatively impact your reach. TikTok does not check IP address when content is published via their official API. However, if you have a brand new account, it's still recommended to publish as a draft first and manually post while warming up the account.

### How long does it take for a draft to appear in TikTok after publishing from ReelFarm?

Usually less than 60 seconds. On rare occasions, due to the TikTok API, it can take up to 30 minutes. This delay is on TikTok's end, not ReelFarm's.

### My post was published to TikTok multiple times (duplicates). Why did this happen?

Duplicate posts typically happen because of a backend DB connection issue where ReelFarm wasn't receiving TikTok's publish status updates, making it appear the post didn't go through when it actually did. If you kept clicking 'Publish' assuming nothing happened, it would re-attempt each time. The scheduling functionality is not affected. If duplicates appear, delete the extra posts from TikTok directly.

### A post shows 'Processing...' and never finishes — what should I do?

The 'Processing...' status means ReelFarm sent the post to TikTok but isn't receiving the status update back (a UI/backend sync issue). The post has likely been published successfully. Check your TikTok app: if it was set to draft, look in Inbox → System Notifications. If it was set to publish immediately, check your TikTok profile. If it's not there, wait a few minutes and check again. If the issue persists, contact support.

### Does posting immediately (vs. as a draft) hurt my views?

Auto-publishing directly is only risky for brand new accounts. If your account is aged (3–6+ months old) with a good posting history and engagement, you can safely auto-publish immediately and still get reach. For new accounts, it's safer to post as a draft and manually publish from the TikTok app while you continue warming up the account.

### How do I set up an automation?

1. Go to the 'Automations' page in ReelFarm.
2. Click 'Create Automation' and go through that flow.

Once set up, the automation runs fully on autopilot — you do not need to do anything daily.

### Do I need to manually feed images and text into my automation every day?

No! You set up your automation once and it runs forever on autopilot. ReelFarm will automatically generate new text content and pull images from your collection (or from the web) each time it creates a new slideshow. You only need to set things up once.

### How do I change the language of my automated slideshows?

1. Go to the 'Automations' page.
2. Click 'Edit' on your automation.
3. Go to the 'Text Content' tab.
4. Click 'Settings (Beta).'
5. Use the 'Language' selector to choose your desired language.

Note: This only affects future slideshows — previously created ones will remain in the original language.

### Does ReelFarm support languages other than English?

Yes! ReelFarm supports all languages. You can configure the language output in your automation settings under Text Content → Settings (Beta) → Language. Supported languages include but are not limited to French, Spanish, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Hungarian, Hebrew, and more.

### How do I change my automation schedule?

Go to the 'Automations' page, click 'Edit' on your automation, and navigate to the 'Schedule' tab. You can set the times and days you want posts to be created. Make sure you select the days of the week before saving — if no days are selected, the schedule will revert to default. You can also update schedules from the 'Home' page's quick-edit view.

### Can I schedule multiple posts per day from one automation?

Yes! On the Growth Plan and above, you can configure an automation to run multiple times per day. In the Schedule tab, add multiple time slots and select which days of the week to post. Make sure to select at least one day before saving, otherwise the schedule won't save properly.

## Hooks & Content

### What are hooks and how do I manage them?

Hooks are the attention-grabbing opening lines of your slideshow (e.g., '5 habits that changed my life', 'things i wish i knew before...'). You can add, edit, and manage your hooks in the 'Automations' page under the 'Hooks' section. You can store many hooks and the automation will rotate through them. If you notice the same hooks being repeated too frequently, this is a known issue that has been flagged for improvement.

### What is the 'Force CTA Slide' feature?

The 'Force CTA Slide' adds a pre-made image slide at the very end of your slideshow as a call-to-action. This slide is expected to already have your CTA text or branding on it — ReelFarm will NOT add generated text on top of it (by design, to avoid covering your CTA). If you want the last slide to have AI-generated text, turn off 'Force CTA Slide' and instead use your prompt to instruct the AI to include a CTA in the final slide.

### What is the 'Soft Sell' feature?

The Soft Sell prompt allows you to subtly mention your product within the slideshow content without it feeling like an ad. You configure this in your automation under the 'Text Content' tab in the 'Soft Sell' prompt field. This is different from a hard CTA — a soft sell casually weaves your product into the content so it feels organic. Make sure your soft sell instructions are in the dedicated 'Soft Sell' field and not buried in the Format prompt.

### How do I add my product context to an automation?

In your automation settings, there is a 'Product Context' or product section where you can describe your product, its benefits, and what you want the AI to know about it. This context is used to generate relevant hooks, slide content, and soft sells. If the AI-generated content isn't accurately representing your product, review and update your product context and format prompt — be very specific about what your product does and doesn't do.

### Why isn't my prompt being followed correctly? The generated content doesn't match my instructions.

A few common reasons:

1. Your format prompt may have conflicting or unclear instructions — be very specific and explicit.
2. Make sure your product context and soft sell instructions are in the right fields (not mixed together).
3. If the AI isn't mentioning your product, ensure your product name and description are clearly stated in the 'Soft Sell CTA' section.
4. For language/styling issues, add explicit instructions like 'ALL text must use Outline style' or 'all text must be capitalized.'

## Images & Collections

### How do I create and manage image collections?

Go to the 'Image Collection' section in the sidebar. You can create a collection and upload your own images to it. These images will then be used by your automations when generating slideshows. Images can be tagged/captioned so the automation can intelligently match images to specific slide content (e.g., if a slide mentions 'pasta,' an image tagged 'pasta' will be used). When you first sign up, ReelFarm automatically creates 2 image collections for you during onboarding.

### Why are my slideshows using the same photos over and over?

The system is designed to avoid reusing the same photos, but if your collection is small, repetition is more likely. Adding more images to your collection reduces repetition.

### Why are some of my slides missing images?

This usually happens because one or more images in your image collection have invalid URLs or are corrupted. The automation cannot process invalid images, so those slides get skipped. To fix it: go to your image collection, identify and delete any images that appear broken or don't load. ReelFarm may also display an error message 'All slides must have images' — refreshing the page often resolves a UI sync issue.

### Can I use images from Pinterest in my slideshows?

Yes! You can use Pinterest images in your slideshows. When creating a slideshow manually in the Slideshow Editor, you can search and select Pinterest photos. If Pinterest images are not showing up, try refreshing the page — this was a known bug that has since been resolved.

### Can images in my collection auto-match to specific slide content?

Yes! You can add captions or tags to images in your collection. When the automation generates a slide about a specific topic (e.g., 'cacio e pepe pasta'), it will look for images in your collection tagged with that topic and use them. If no matching tagged image is found, it will default to picking a random image from your collection.

### How do I source photos for slideshows?

Great sources for aesthetic photos: Tumblr and Pinterest (both group similar aesthetic images in blogs/boards, making it easy to find cohesive sets). Search keywords related to your niche on these platforms. You can also use your own photos, AI-generated images, or product screenshots. For automated slideshows, upload images to an Image Collection in ReelFarm.

## Slideshow Editor & Export

### How do I generate slideshows manually (without an automation)?

You can create slideshows manually in the 'Slideshow Editor' page. From there you can write your own prompt, choose hooks, select images from your collections or search online, and customize the layout. Both the manual editor and the automation system are available — you can use either or both.

### How do I edit a slideshow after it's been generated?

Go to the 'Slideshow Editor' page. You can find your generated slideshows there or in the 'Library' page. From the editor, you can edit text items, change text styling (font size, color, position, outline), swap images, add or remove slides, and re-export.

### How do I customize the text style in slideshows (size, position, color, outline)?

You can control text styling through your automation's format prompt. In your format prompt, you can specify: the number of text items per slide, the placement of text (e.g., 'centered,' 'top 1/3rd of screen'), font sizes (XXS, XS, S, M, L, XL), word length per item, text width (full, 90%, 80%, 70%, 60%, 50%, etc), and text style (e.g. outline, white text, black text, white background, light white background, black background, light black background, etc). You can also manually edit text styles directly in the Slideshow Editor for individual slideshows.

### How do I export a slideshow?

In the Slideshow Editor, click the large 'Export' button. This creates your final slideshow and deducts 1 export credit. After exporting, you can download the individual slide images as a ZIP file, download the slideshow as a video file, or publish directly to TikTok. You can view and re-download your exported slideshows on both the Slideshow Editor page and the Library page.

### Can I export a slideshow as a video instead of images?

Yes! When exporting a slideshow, click the settings icon next to the Export button and toggle on 'Export as Video.' You can also specify the duration (in seconds) for each slide when exporting as video. This is useful for posting on platforms like YouTube Shorts or Instagram Reels.

### Where can I find and download my exported slideshows?

Your exported slideshows are available in two places: (1) The 'Slideshow Editor' page — scroll down below the editor to see your exports. (2) The 'Library' page — this shows all your exported slideshows. For greenscreen memes, go to the 'Greenscreen Memes' editor page and scroll down — memes are not shown on the main Library page.

### Can I create slideshows without using an automation?

Yes! You can use the Slideshow Editor to manually create slideshows. You write or paste your own hook/prompt, choose your images, and customize each slide individually. Both the manual Slideshow Editor and the automated Automation system are available simultaneously.

### Can I write my own text for slideshows manually?

Yes. In the Slideshow Editor, you can manually type and edit the text on each individual slide. The editor allows you to customize text content, styling, images, and layout slide by slide.

### Does ReelFarm support video slideshows (not just image carousels)?

Yes. When exporting a slideshow, you can toggle 'Export as Video' to export your slideshow as an MP4 video file instead of individual images. You can also specify the duration (in seconds) for each slide.

### Can I import a slideshow from the library and change only the images?

The ability to import a slideshow from the library and replace only the images (keeping the text) is being built and coming very soon.

## Troubleshooting

### How long does slideshow generation take?

For automations, a slideshow typically takes ~60 seconds to generate and export. On the high end (if you have 10+, grid images like 2:2, and are auto-pulling images from the internet), it can take up to 3-5 minutes. If generation is timing out, try simplifying your prompt or reducing the number of slides and images per slide.

### What should I do if my slideshow generation keeps timing out?

Try the following:

1. Simplify your prompt — very long or complex prompts can cause timeouts.
2. Reduce the number of slides or images per slide.
3. Use images from your own collection instead of auto-pulling from the internet.
4. Refresh the page and try again.

If the issue persists, contact support — it may be a backend issue that needs to be fixed.

### Why is the text preview in the editor not matching the exported output?

The rendered text in the final export may appear slightly larger or positioned differently than in the editor preview. If text is wrapping to a new line in the export but not in the preview, try using a smaller font size or shortening the text.

### Why is text overlapping on my slides?

Text overlap usually happens when the automation doesn't know how many text items you want per slide, and generates too many. In your automation's format prompt, explicitly specify the number of text items per slide (e.g., 'Each slide should have exactly 2 text items: one title and one body'). You can also specify font sizes and positioning to prevent overlap.

## Content Strategy & Growth

### What is the best slideshow format to use?

The most versatile and consistently high-performing slideshow format is the listicle (ordered list) format. Examples include hooks like '5 habits that...', '7 ways to...', '3 foods that...', 'What I learned from...'. This format works because it's evergreen, provides clear value, and naturally encourages saves and shares — which signals quality content to TikTok's algorithm. The key is to make each slide feel like a bullet point in a valuable list.

### What should my strategy be with ReelFarm?

ReelFarm is built for creating valuable, evergreen content in your niche at scale, with the goal of driving organic traffic back to your product or website. The recommended strategy is:

1. Create consistent, valuable slideshow content that people want to save or share.
2. Soft-sell your product subtly within the content rather than making every post a hard ad.
3. Post consistently (1–3x/day).
4. Use a link in bio or mention your product name/website within the content.
5. Keep using TikTok organically (scroll, engage, comment) alongside your automated posting.

### How often should I post?

In the first 1–2 weeks of a new account, don't post more than 1–2 times per day, or every other day. If your posts are consistently getting reach after 2 weeks, you can scale to 1–3x per day. Consistency is more important than volume — 1 post per day every day outperforms 3 posts per day for a week followed by nothing. For very new accounts, avoid exceeding 2–3 posts per day.

### How do I warm up a TikTok account?

Warming up an account means using TikTok like a real human consumer, so TikTok's algorithm recognizes you as an authentic user before you start posting. To warm up:

1. Scroll your For You Page (FYP) for 5-20 minutes per day.
2. Like, comment, follow, bookmark, and engage with content in your niche.
3. Do NOT post during the first 3–7 days — just consume and engage.
4. After 3–7 days, post manually 1x per day, but continue scrolling/engaging daily for at least 5–10 minutes.

The key is to maintain a genuine relationship with the platform even after you start posting. The most common issue with users getting 0 views is neglecting this step and being impatient. It's more important than people realize.

### How long do I need to warm up my account?

For a brand new account or one that hasn't been used in a while, warm it up for at least 5–7 days before posting (some recommend 3 days minimum, but longer is better). If you already use an account daily for personal TikTok scrolling and never post, you don't need to warm it up before starting.

### I'm getting 0 views on my posts — what should I do?

Zero views is always an account issue. Ask yourself: Am I posting more than I'm scrolling/engaging? Is my account new or recently inactive? Here's what to do:

1. Stop posting temporarily.
2. Spend 10-20 minutes per day scrolling and engaging on the FYP for 5–7 days.
3. Then post once. You should see at least 150–300 views.
4. Continue scrolling daily, and post once every other day initially.
5. Gradually increase to 1x per day.

Avoid posting 3+ times per day on a new or recovering account.

### How do I sell my product in these posts?

There are two main approaches:

1. **Soft Sell** — Mention your product subtly and naturally within the content so it doesn't feel like an ad. The viewer feels like they discovered your product themselves.
2. **Hard CTA** — Explicitly tell viewers what to do (e.g., 'Click the link in bio,' 'Find us at website.com,' 'Download [App Name] on the App Store').

A link in bio helps conversions but isn't required. Many creators prefer the soft sell approach for organic content because it preserves the authenticity of the post.

### Can I use ReelFarm slideshows for TikTok ads?

You can run ads using slideshows, but only if you own the rights to all images used. Using images sourced from Pinterest, Tumblr, or other sites without permission for paid advertising can create legal exposure. If you plan to run ads, use your own original images or images you have licensed for commercial use.

### How fast can I get to 1,000+ views per post?

If you're consistently getting 500–1,000 views, you're already doing things right — it's a 'learn, apply, repeat' game from there. Identify which hooks and narratives perform better than others. When you find a format that works, double down and create more like it. Analyze your best-performing posts to understand what resonated and replicate that pattern.

### Where do I find ideas for slideshows?

Several good sources:

1. ReelFarm's slideshow database at reel.farm/database — filter by niche and most viewed.
2. TikTok itself — search your niche and bookmark every slideshow you find interesting. The more you engage with slideshow content on TikTok, the more TikTok will show you similar content, giving you a steady stream of inspiration.
3. Look for patterns you can repurpose across niches — many formats work universally.

### Can I use ReelFarm for B2B businesses?

You can, but TikTok may not be the ideal platform for all B2B businesses. If your business requires speaking to a sales rep or targets a professional audience, platforms like LinkedIn and X (Twitter) are likely more effective. ReelFarm's slideshows can also be exported and repurposed for those platforms. For B2C or consumer-facing products, TikTok and ReelFarm's automation are a great fit.

### Can slideshows work for my Shopify app or SaaS product?

Absolutely. Example hooks that work well: '0 to $10k in 30 days (here's my Shopify stack)' — list 5 tools you use, one being your app. Or '3 hard truths I learned growing a $10k MRR store' — make one of those lessons a problem your app solves. The key: frame your hook as a list, make each slide a valuable bullet point, and include your product as a natural part of the story rather than an outright ad.

AI Avatars (available on the 'AI Avatars' page) let you generate photorealistic AI-generated characters using text prompts (powered by Higgsfield's Soul model). You can then use these avatars as images in your slideshows or for Hook + Demo videos. Generated avatars use AI Credits. To use an avatar in a slideshow, create an Image Collection, upload your avatar images to it, and reference that collection in your automation.

### What are Greenscreen Memes?

Greenscreen Memes are a content format available in ReelFarm. They are accessible on the 'Greenscreen Memes' editor page. You can download them or publish them to TikTok from there.

### Can I use my own custom AI model (e.g., Claude, Gemini) for text generation?

Currently, there is no option to plug in your own AI model via API. ReelFarm uses its own AI model selection for text generation. If you're experiencing poor output quality for a specific language, contact support.

### What is the Slideshow Database / Library?

The ReelFarm slideshow database (available at https://reel.farm/database) is a collection of example slideshows created by real pages posting to TikTok. You can browse it by niche, filter by most viewed, and get inspiration for your own content. It's a great way to see what formats and hooks are working well across different industries.
